places in genet

genet auditorium

built in 1938

The Genet school Auditorium is a very big auditorium that is used for lots of purposes. Including school assemblies, plays, meeting with special people, and videos too.

genet library

built in 1939

The Genet school Library is a very vast library and has lots of books to read. The library holds a book fair 2 times each year. Kids can come and buy books and toys at the book fair. The library also has computers and very accurate checkout system too.

genet gym

built in 1940

The Genet school Gym is huge and is a gym where lots of fun games are played. The gym has been used for presentations and tests too.

achievements

Souper Bowl
genet soup drive

The Genet soup drive is very big and lots of donations of cans are received. It is called the Souper Bowl because it is held during the time of the Super Bowl of football in February.

Ronald McDonald House Charities
Big charity that is very helpful

The Ronald McDonald House charity is where you read and a sponsor fills out a book log and has to pay for ever day that you read. The Ronald McDonald House is where sick children as patients can stay close to with their families, home cooked meals and fun games too.

what's new

  • Running Club

    Genet is excited to share that we will begin holding our Genet Jaguars Running Club. Running Club is where you come to school early and run on the track to improve your running. Running Club is free to-do and was stopped due to Covid. Genet is starting Running Club after a very long time and is very hopeful that many students would join.

    • Full-Time School

      Genet is allowing kids through K-2 to go to school 5 days a week. Genet is very close to achieving it's normal state of school but will always keep the rules of Covid. Allowing kids to return to school fully means no distraction and more social interaction. You get to play more games in gym, go on the playground and more sculptures in art too!
